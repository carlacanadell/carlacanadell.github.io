About Me

Carla Canadell is a fashion designer specializing in knitwear and textile design. With a Bachelor in Fashion Design in Barcelona, and a Master’s degree in Textile Design/Knitting Design from the Royal Academy of Fine Arts in Brussels, Belgium, she has worked in Haute Couture at Iris van Herpen in Amsterdam, participating in the Architectonics collection. She now works in bridal fashion in Barcelona.

Interested in Fashion History and Art History, she is usually inspired by historical art movements or characters. Passionate for visual and conceptual research, she translates her ideas through textile experimentation, knitting, volumes, and various techniques.
